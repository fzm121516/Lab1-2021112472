The storm raged for days, tossing the small sailboat like a toy in a bathtub.
Rain lashed against the windows, and the wind howled like a banshee. 
Inside, the three passengers huddled together, their faces pale with fear.
Suddenly, a monstrous wave crashed over the deck, smashing the mast and sending the boat spinning out of control. 
When the water finally subsided, the vessel lay broken and battered, adrift in a vast, unknown sea.
